# Privacy Policy — FastGem → ID Theft Policy Extension **Last updated:** 2026-02-18 -This Chrome extension is an internal tool used by authorized staff to assist with issuing ID Theft Protection policies. ## What the extension does - Reads customer details from FastGem work order pages. - Opens the ID Theft Policy web app and pre-fills the form with those details. - Helps staff copy the resulting policy number back into FastGem. ## Data the extension processes The extension may process: - Name - Email address - Phone number - Address (street, city, state, ZIP) - Plan tier selection ## How data is used Data is used only to: - Pre-fill the ID Theft Policy web app form - Submit the policy issuance request to Enfortra (Identity Protection Services) to generate a policy number ## Data storage - The extension does not sell or share data for advertising. - The extension does not store customer data long-term. - Any temporary data used for prefill is held only during the session to complete the workflow. ## Third-party services Policy issuance is performed through Enfortra services. Customer data is sent to Enfortra only for policy issuance.(Im just calling it's API) ## Security Access to this tool is intended for authorized staff only. Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect data during processing. ## Contact If you have questions about this policy, contact: - Email: hiyan@nationalsalespartners.com - Organization: National Sales Partners
